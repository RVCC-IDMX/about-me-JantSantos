Jant Ericson Santos

Raritan, NJ

Hello! My name is Jant Ericson Santos. I migrated from the Philippines a year ago and to permanently stay in America. I studied a year of undergraduate degree on Civil Engineering back in the Philippines but decided to pursue Information technology. Ever since I was little, computers have always sparked curiousity inside me.

I used to compete in VALORANT back in the Philippines. Playing competitively was a big passion of mine because the grind to improve as a player, the adrenaline during the matches, having fun with playing with my teammates motivated me. That mindset of constant improvement has carried over even in different aspects of my life

Now, I'm not really sure yet what field I want to focus on but one thing is for sure that I want to better my knowledge in computers, graduate, and make my parents proud.

Hobbies:

  • I love playing video games in my free time, and I still grind VALORANT a little bit while competing in small tournaments here and there.
  • I go to the gym around five times a week because I enjoy it, and I'm working on becoming stronger and healthier.
  • I enjoy going on road trips and watching the scenery because I find it beautiful and relaxing.
  • I watch TV shows and movies during my leisure time as another way to unwind.
  • My family and I like to go out on weekends to spend time together, stroll around, and enjoy family bonding.
